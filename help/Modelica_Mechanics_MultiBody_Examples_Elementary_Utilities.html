Modelica.Mechanics.MultiBody.Examples.Elementary.Utilities.theoreticalNormalGravityWGS84

WGS84 normal gravity over earth ellipsoid in negative y-direction

Information




Function that computes the theoretical gravity of the
WGS84 ellipsoid earth model at and close to the earth ellipsoid surface, for the
given "geodeticLatitude" and the given "height=r[2]" over the
ellipsoid surface.





Extends from Modelica.Mechanics.MultiBody.Interfaces.partialGravityAcceleration.
