Modelica.Electrical.QuasiStationary.MultiPhase.Sensors.VoltageQuasiRMSSensor

Continuous quasi voltage RMS sensor for multi phase system

Information

This sensor determines the continuous quasi RMS value of a multi phase voltage system, representing an equivalent RMS voltage vector I or phasor. 

 V = sqrt(sum(v[k]^2 for k in 1:m)/m)
