.Modelica.Mechanics.MultiBody.Frames.axis

Information

Syntax

e = Frames.axis(axis);

Description

A unit vector e is returned depending on whether x-, y-, or z-axis is required.

 axis  |    e
-------+-----------
  1    |  {1,0,0}
  2    |  {1,0,0}
  3    |  {1,0,0}
